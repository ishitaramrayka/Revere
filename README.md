## 💡 What Is Revere?

Revere is a smart glasses concept for Alzheimer's care that combines AI assistance with caregiver support tools.

It is designed to help reduce confusion in day-to-day life through four core ideas:

### 👤 Facial Recognition & Relationship Prompting
Recognizes familiar faces from a caregiver-managed gallery and provides subtle audio prompts to help users identify loved ones, friends, or caregivers.

### ⏰ Routine Coaching & Medication Reminders
Delivers scheduled reminders and guidance throughout the day, helping users stay on track with routines and medication.

### 📍 Wandering Detection & Location Awareness
Supports safety with context-aware location assistance and caregiver-facing alerts for potentially risky or unfamiliar movement.

### 🧑‍💻 Caregiver Dashboard Integration
Allows caregivers to manage face profiles, routines, reminders, and alerts through a companion dashboard experience.

🧠 Design Philosophy

Revere’s landing page was built around three ideas:

1. Calm, Not Clinical

The product addresses a serious healthcare challenge, but the experience should still feel warm, reassuring, and human.

2. Show the Product Before the Stack

People should understand why it matters before they learn how it was built.

3. Motion as Narrative

Animations are used to:

guide attention
shape pacing
reinforce product value
make the concept feel tangible

This is not just a static site. It is a storytelling experience.

🌍 Why This Project Matters

Alzheimer’s care is not only a technical challenge.
It is also a challenge of:

memory
orientation
independence
safety
familiarity
dignity

Revere explores how AI, wearable technology, and caregiver tools can support those moments in a way that feels subtle and human.

The goal is not to replace care.
The goal is to make care feel lighter, calmer, and more continuous.
